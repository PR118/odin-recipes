Potato Soup

Description

Using this recipe you will be able to make a creamy potato soup

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place bacon in large skillet cooking at medium-high heat until done. Next put bacon slices on paper towels to drain grease.
  3. While doing Step 2, melt butter over medium heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well-mixed. Slowly pour in milk, whisking until consistency is smooth and thick.
  4. Add your potatoes and onions and bring to boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Next stir in your crumbled bacon, cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper. Continue cooking and stirring until cheese melts.
  6. After these steps are completed your dish will be complete.
