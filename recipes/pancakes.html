Pancakes

Description

This recipe will contain the ingredients and steps you will need to take to make Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. First combine your flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a large bowl. Beat together buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter in a seperate bowl.
  2. Heat lightly oiled pan over medium-high heat
  3. Next pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture;use a wooden spoon or fork to mix until it's just blended. The batter will be a bit lumpy
  4. Now poor your batter onto your preheated pan. Cook until bubbles appear on surface of pancake. After 1-2 minutes, flip with spatula and cook until golden brown.
